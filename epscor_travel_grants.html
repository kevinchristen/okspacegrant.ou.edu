Travel Grant Solicitations
**All travel must be domestic only. Travel Grant funds may not be used for international travel.

Up to ten Travel Grant Awards, for a maximum of $2,500, are awarded for faculty to spend up to three days and two nights at a NASA Center visiting with scientists. Faculty may include students in travel. The purpose of this grant is to provide the first step in initiating contact and forging a direct partnership with a NASA Center to develop possible research projects. Please refer to NASA's Three Mission Directorates or solicitations from the Office of the Chief Technologist for areas of research interest.

To apply, submit the following information:
  1. A cover/application page.
    (Your specific research area and your emphasis on one of NASA's Three Mission Directorates or a solicitation from the Office of the Chief Technologist must be included on the application page).
  2. A budget (using the provided form).
  3. A one-page abstract.
  4. A letter of interest from a NASA center counterpart.
Applications and cover pages must be sent electronically to OKSG.EPSCoR@ou.edu and adminOKSG@ou.edu no later than close of business November 13, 2012.

Awards will be announced by November 30, 2012.

**All travel must be completed by March 31, 2013.
