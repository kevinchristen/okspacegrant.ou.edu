Women in Science
What's this?
Women in Science is a workshop aimed to educate students at all levels by encouraging and supporting interdisciplinary, multi-disciplinary research experiences, and education programs. Each year at this event, college students volunteer for this one day conference, which includes middle school and high school attendees from all over Oklahoma. Each volunteer receives a $100 fellowship.
Past Women in Science workshops
The Space Grant Consortium has participated in this event since 2009. For Women in Science 2011, there was a total of 40 volunteers and about 200 student and teacher attendees.
