The OSGC Staff
Victoria Snowden - Director
Dr. Victoria Snowden was appointed director of the Oklahoma NASA Space Grant Consortium/ NASA EPSCoR in 1991. Prior to her appointment as OK Space Grant Director, she served as Director of Education for the United States Space Foundation in the mid 1980’s. Dr. Snowden earned her undergraduate and graduate degrees from the University of Northern Colorado. She received her M.A. and Ed.D. in Aerospace Education from the University of Northern Colorado, and her B.S. in Elementary Education from University of Colorado.
Dr. Snowden is also on the Executive Board of National Council of Space Grant Directors. She served third year as Executive Board Member to the Coalition for the Advancement of Science and Mathematics Education (CASMEO). She continued as Vice President to the Board of STARBASE Oklahoma. She also continued to play an active role on the Linking Leaders Advisory Committee. She completed fourth year of appointment to National Council of Space Grant Directors Education Panel, and completed third year on Aeronautics Working Group of National Council of Space Grant Directors.
Madeline Baugher - Program Coordinator
Madeline Baugher joined Oklahoma Space Grant Consortium in 2007 when she was appointed Intuitional Representative for Southwestern Oklahoma State University. In August of 2010, she was appointed Program Coordinator for the Consortium. Her responsibilities include data compilation necessary for reporting and other tasks as assigned by the Director. She has taught in the Computer Science Department at Southwestern Oklahoma State University since 1979. Her Master’s of Computer Science degree is from Texas A&M University.
Kelly Goodrich - Administrative Coordinator
Kelly Goodrich comes to OSGC from the world of finance. Her career in banking began over 30 years ago, and has experience primarily at banks, but has also served as compliance director for a credit union and in the hotel accounting field. Her varied experience in administration and management has prepared her for the many facets of the position of administrative coordinator.
Kat Rigby - Technology Intern
Kat Rigby, our current Technology Intern, is in charge of programming the website for OSGC. She is a junior at the University of Oklahoma studying computer science. She has been with the Space Grant since May of 2011.
